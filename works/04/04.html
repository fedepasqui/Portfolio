    THE CHALLENGE

    Portray the history of the 20th century
    in a different way


    ROLE

    Design concept, Editorial content, Layout design

    The 20th century was all about change, mostly because of the historical tragedies and big social, economical and civil conquests. What a better way to tell the story about this change than travel?
    Not only as a metaphor of this big change, but also because it’s when travel originated
    and developed as we know
    it today.

    The timeline is visually represented as a unique line in each opening chapter,
    which eventually ends up in the front cover, shaping the world where
    we live in today.

    The travel of the 20th century is concluded,
    but we’re still tracing this line, that’s why it is
    so important to know
    the different events
    of this long journey.
