    ALSO ON

    Behance

    I love traveling and exploring new places,
    but I also love my hometown. I wanted to show how beautiful and special Bergamo is, even if it’s smaller and less known than other Italian cities. Sadly, it is now known for one of the most hit city by COVID-19, but this should not be the reason for Bergamo to be famous.

    This leaflet is designed to communicate
    its peculiarity for whoever is not even aware of this place, and for whoever lives there but takes it for granted, like I did once. Curing this research
    I even found out new things that made me
    love this city even more.
