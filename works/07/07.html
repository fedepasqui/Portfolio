    ROLE

    Video concept, direction and editing

    Now more than ever, with a general anti-immigration sentiment
    rising and the war in Ucraine, there’s a problem with accepting the
    diversity. I wanted to talk about it and raise awareness through
    design by telling why cultural exchange is so important to
    overcome this issue. This not only by telling the fun parts
    of the Erasmus, but also by sharing other testimonies from students
    who lived this experience and my reflection that stresses its impact
    on the people.
